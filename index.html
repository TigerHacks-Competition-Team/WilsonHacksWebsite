About Us

Wilson Hacks is a 12-hour Hackathon for any middle or high school hackers! At the hackathon, you will work with other students to learn how to code and work on other projects. After 12 hours of hacking, you will showcase their ideas to a panel of judges and compete for prizes. We hope to see you there!
